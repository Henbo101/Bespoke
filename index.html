01. Brand Strategy & Positioning

We build architectural adventures.

For design-conscious families who refuse to compromise on the aesthetic of their home, Bespoke Garden Play stands apart. We don't just build climbing frames; we build architectural adventures. Handcrafted luxury climbing frames with ply-lined interiors and cedar shingle roofs. Designed in the UK to outlast childhood.

The Archetype

The Creator meets The Magician. We provide the flawless, Creator-driven structure, so the child can provide the Magician-driven wonder. Every structure is a masterclass in craftsmanship, safety, and style.

The Pillars

  • I. Handbuilt in the UK: Handcrafted by skilled carpenters in our workshop.
  • II. Built to Outlast: We use only the finest, sustainably sourced, pressure-treated timber.
  • III. The "Real House" Philosophy: We are pioneers of the ply-lined interior.
02. Verbal Identity & Messaging

Not just a playhouse. A piece of your home.

Our voice is the literary equivalent of a beautifully tailored linen suit: relaxed but impeccably structured. We are understated, evocative, authoritative, and timeless. We don't need to look down on high-street alternatives; our craftsmanship speaks for itself.

  • The Lexicon

    Words We Embrace: Playhouse, Playcentre, Climbing Frame, Play Castle, Architectural Adventures, The Signature Series, The Masterpiece Collection.

    Words We Retire: Flat-pack, Shed, Plastic, Cheap, High-Street Alternatives. We do not compare; we simply state our excellence.

  • The Pitch

    "Our collection of handcrafted luxury playhouses and climbing frames is meticulously designed for the garden and built to inspire the imagination. With over 25 years of experience crafting the most extraordinary and unique wooden structures, we have mastered the art of creating outdoor spaces where adventure, creativity and fun begin."

  • The Modular Journey

    We don't "upsell"; we evolve. "Start Small. Dream Big. Grow Often." We discuss "Modular Extensions" and building a "Grand Estate" that evolves with the family.

04. Art Direction & Photography

Atmosphere over architecture.

We sell the feeling of owning a Bespoke playhouse. Real photography is tightly cropped to show craft; AI imagery builds the faceless, tactile lifestyle world.

Real Photography

Abstract the structure. Emphasise the "Real House" philosophy by cropping tightly into the ply-lined interior or the Western Red Cedar shingles. Pull down saturation to match the Heritage Palette.

AI Generation Prompts

Strictly faceless posing. Focus on tactile textures. Lighting must be overcast British daylight, golden hour, or dappled canopy light.

05. The Grid & Layout System

Alabaster Space.

Empty space is an active design element. If a layout feels cramped, the perceived value of the product drops immediately.

  • Asymmetric Balance

    We abandon simple centred content. Balance a massive, sweeping headline with a tiny, precisely aligned paragraph spanning just a few columns.

  • Left-Aligned Strictness

    Almost all typography must be left-aligned with a "ragged right" edge. Centred text feels static; left-alignment feels confident and architectural.

  • The Baseline Anchor

    Elements across a page should visually anchor to the same invisible horizontal lines at their top or bottom edges.
